Wolves

Wolves are found in North America, Europe, Asia and North Africa.

Wolves eat:

  1. Deer
  2. Elk
  3. Bison
  4. Moose

Wolves live and hunt in packs of around six to ten animals. They are known to roam large distances, perhaps 12 miles in a single day.
